B·FLIX STUDIO

A dual-track pixel animation machine. Two film loops composite into one picture in real time.

A
VIDEO TRACK — Load a video or photo sequence. It loops independently as the base layer.
B
CODE TRACK — Write BEFLIX-64 pixel commands or ask Claude AI to animate the 64×36 grid. Loops independently from A.
C
DRAW TRACK — Hand-draw on frames. Unlike A and B, drawings are per-frame—they don't loop.

If A has 48 frames and B has 4 frames, B loops 12 times while A plays once. Hit PLAY to see them composite. Loop seam lines appear in the timeline.
