# TAX-FUTURE

**Taxonomy of Futures Engine Archive** — 26 prototypes exploring how the Stuart Candy / Dunne & Raby nested cone model (Possible, Plausible, Probable, Preferable) can become an operative, interactive, morphable instrument rather than a static diagram.

## Families

### Fig 4.7 Diagram Series (5 files)
The core compilation engine. Each variant implements a four-phase morph from isolated radial origins through vector compression and composite staging into a final superposition. Differences lie in the Phase 4 target geometry: stratigraphic strata (`a.html`), radial collapse (`b.html`), flat-capped composite (`c.html`), sector collapse (`d.html`), or the master discrete-to-superposition tool (`taxonomy-06.html`).

### SCOT Timeline (4 files)
Instruments grounded in the Social Construction of Technology (SCOT) framework, mapping the 1885–1890 bicycle controversy. Four Relevant Social Groups (Industrialists, Traditionalists, Sporting Elite, Pragmatic Commuters) compete for interpretive flexibility until technological closure stabilizes the Safety Bicycle. Prototypes include editable archival records, HUD tooltips, and haptic audio.

### Taxonomy Series (6 files)
The iterative development lineage from genesis (`taxonomy-00`) through area-conservation (`taxonomy-01`), superimposed slices (`taxonomy-02`), holographic compilation (`taxonomy-03`), to the 2D Phase Space Matrix (`taxonomy-07`). Each iteration introduced one foundational principle: color palette, Archimedes unrolling, layering, pipeline, and finally dual-axis crosshair navigation.

### MNTAF Bicycle (3 files)
The Multi-level Network of Technology, Actors, and Futures framework. Maps 30+ historical bicycle variants onto taxonomy cones using BDI (Belief-Desire-Intent) reasoning. The key insight: every bicycle form sits inside multiple future cones simultaneously — the taxonomy becomes an interpretive lens, not a prediction tool.

### Sensorama / VR (2 files)
The most content-rich prototypes. 64+ named units mapping the Sensorama's lineage across Pressure, Source, Function, and Form categories. Inline SVG apparatus illustrations (headset, cabinet, simulator, scent diffuser, body, screen) animate through a four-phase topology (Separate Layers → Strips → Composite Wheel → Persistent Cone).

### Canvas Renderers (4 files)
Prototypes using HTML Canvas rather than SVG. Includes the stacked interference engine (dual-axis drag for morph + cone separation), the dark-mode quad-slice landscape, the compact parallel geometry, and the area-invariance sector instrument (the core geometric proof).

### Epistemic Flow (3 files)
Counter-factual timeline instruments. Extend the taxonomy with epistemic reasoning: each slice represents not just a future prediction but an entire epistemic stance — a way of knowing what could have been.

## Core Principles
- **Area Invariance**: Archimedes-unrolled geometry ensures interpretive space stays constant during morphing
- **Refraction over Evolution**: Artifacts are defined by their refraction across multiple cones, not a single evolutionary line
- **Closure Narrative**: All morphs terminate in technological closure — one form dominates all trajectories
