COLOSSEUM

Four Concepts × Four Native Geospatial Languages

I

Well-Known Text

Raw geometry primitives on a Cartesian coordinate grid. LINESTRING arcs, POLYGON rings, GEOMETRYCOLLECTION assemblies. No projection, no metadata — pure topology.

RENDERER: SVG COORDINATE GRID
II

GeoJSON — FeatureCollection

WGS84 coordinates on an interactive slippy map. Real lon/lat centered on 41.8902°N, 12.4923°E. Properties carry conceptual metadata and operative meaning.

RENDERER: LEAFLET.JS INTERACTIVE MAP
III

CityGML — LOD1 3D City Model

Three-dimensional MultiSurface geometry with real z-heights (0–57m). GenericCityObject attributes encode material layers and temporal depth. Drag to orbit.

RENDERER: THREE.JS WEBGL 3D
IV

KML — Keyhole Markup Language

Google Earth placemarks with aaBGR color styles, MultiGeometry composition, and gx:Tour animated camera paths. Styled visualization with tour trajectory overlay.

RENDERER: STYLED PLACEMARKS + CAMERA TOUR PATH
