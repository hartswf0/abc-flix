Arc Tunnel Controls

The tube is the edit. Time flows outward through the tube. Tracks are rails. Clips are collars.

Gestures

  • Tap clip to select.
  • Drag M to move time.
  • Drag H or T to trim.
  • Drag L to change rail.
  • Drag UP to lift inside, seam, outside.
  • Drag R to roll.
  • Drag OUT to route.
  • Drag ring to scrub.
